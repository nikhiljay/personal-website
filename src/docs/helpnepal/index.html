Thousands Dead.

The 2015 Nepal earthquake, also known as the "Himalayan earthquake", has killed at least 7,000 people and injured more than twice as many. The earthquake also triggered an avalanche on Mount Everest, killing at least 19 and trapping hundreds, making it the deadliest day on the mountain in history.
